About Us

We specialize in the transportation of oil and heavy machinery, ensuring safe and timely delivery to destinations across the globe. With decades of experience, our commitment to quality and safety remains unmatched. We were established on the 6th of November 2017, and are headquartered in Abuja. We are a robust and efficiently structured organisation with a tradition of discipline and customer satisfaction. We are structured with a staff of 14: 8 on-field workers and 6 office workers. El Rohi integrated service prides itself on its versatility and result-oriented approach. El Rohi integrated Services limited has an active underground presence as we Have undertaken numerous logistic and supply projects for the private sector. We are also into offshore support platform work.
